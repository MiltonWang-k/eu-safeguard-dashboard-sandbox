Methodology

Professional definitions without changing your homepage terminology.

YoY change

Compare the same product category, quarter, and country / quota pool against the prior-year quarter only when the quota path is comparable.

Access-change badge

Show a badge only when the access basis changes, such as a new quota pool, an added or removed country-specific quota, or another structure change that makes direct YoY comparison misleading.

Quota-pool tooltip

Keep an asterisk on FTA Quota CSQ, FTA Quota Other countries, and Other countries. You can put Annex eligibility wording in the tooltip.
